ABOUT SOMA'S CREATION

Based in Asansol, West Bengal, Soma’s Creation was founded by its namesake, Soma Chatterjee, who is an approachable and enthusiastic entrepreneur balancing home and business like a rockstar.

Soma’s Creation was founded in 2018 from one spare room in her house catering to just friends and family. In the last 2.5 years, it has grown to stunning heights with an online customer base of more than 30,000 people, shipping across India.

All this was possible not only through the perseverance of Ms Chatterjee, but also because of the unflinching support of initial and long-standing customers across India. The success of the business solely depends on our service, quality of our products and genuine prices.
